Branding

Branding is the process of creating a unique identity for a business, product, or individual through visual elements, messaging, and overall perception. It encompasses logos, color schemes, typography, and tone of communication to establish a distinct and memorable presence. A strong brand builds trust, fosters customer loyalty, and differentiates an entity from its competitors. Effective branding ensures consistency across all platforms, reinforcing the values and mission behind the brand while shaping how the audience perceives and interacts with it.
